About the Rotary Club of Northeast Seattle

We serve Lake City! Our projects are mostly local because we like to interact with the people we serve. Last year our members raised $3000 and gave a hundred hours of service to Thanksgiving and Christmas Food Baskets. We also raise money for Local Scholarships, Local Scouting organizations, as well as contributing to Rotary International's goal of world-wide Polio eradication. Northeast Seattle Rotary has also contributed service hours to North Helpline and the Lake City Kiddie Parade.


If you want to have a good time and contribute to a good cause while enjoying a few frosty cold ones, come join us for Bingo Karaoke!
